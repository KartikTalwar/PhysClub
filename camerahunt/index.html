Rules

  • Teams of 2-4 science students must be registered no later than 5 PM on Friday November 16.
  • All picture submissions must be in by 5:30 PM the same day. Any submissions submitted later will not be accepted/
  • If a team picture is not submitted, no points will be given to that team.
  • Submissions can be made from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM on Friday November 16.
  • The list of items will be posted before 8:30 AM on this webpage.
  • The team earning the highest point score will receive $20 in retail services gift card.
