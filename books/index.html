Borrow a Book

There are a number of books in the Physics Club room available for an extended borrowing - until someone else requests to borrow the book, or, if no one else requests the book, until the end of the term. There is a sign-out sheet beside the bookshelf in PhysClub where you can write your name, email and book title and then take the book. If the book you are looking for is out, contact Dakota St. Laurent, "Sketchy" Steve Dawson or any "official" member of PhysClub to request a book. The borrower will be contacted ASAP.
